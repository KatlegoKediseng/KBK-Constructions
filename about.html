Company Overview

Reagile Construction Company, based in Gaborone, Botswana, has been a leading construction firm for the past five years. With a passion for building – or as we say in Setswana, "reagile" – and a strong commitment to quality, we specialize in a wide range of projects, including building construction, civil construction, and electrical construction. As CEO, it was my vision to root our company's identity in our local heritage, hence the name "Reagile," symbolizing the very essence of our work. We pride ourselves on our dedication to client satisfaction, innovative solutions, and sustainable practices, transforming the landscape of our communities.
